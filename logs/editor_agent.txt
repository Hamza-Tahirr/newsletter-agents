Policy and Regulatory Shifts:
'The Game Changer: New Policies in Carbon Credit Market' - The recent policy and regulatory changes by the U.S. government, particularly the rules for the voluntary use of carbon credits, are set to bolster the integrity of the carbon credit market (Reuters Article). Market Dynamics:
'Renewed Confidence in Carbon Credit Markets: A Closer Look' - The combination of regulatory changes and the carbon credit industry's initiative to enhance market integrity has led to a restoration of market confidence (Climate Change News Article).
